# Eigenstate 2 Regional Tasks

This guide covers the Regional category in QoreChain Eigenstate 2.

Observed category size: 12 tasks.

Regional tasks usually involve Korean, Japanese, Vietnamese, Indonesian, Chinese diaspora, or other regional community platforms and blogs.

## Purpose

The goal of Regional tasks is to support multilingual and regional awareness while respecting local platform rules and community expectations.

## Common Task Types

| Task Type | Typical Proof | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Regional community post | Public post or community link | Use the requested region and platform |
| Translation article | Medium/blog link | Keep translation accurate and source-aware |
| Forum or blog post | Public article/post link | Avoid machine-like low-quality text |
| Telegram/KakaoTalk sharing | Public channel link or screenshot proof | Private groups may be weak proof |
| Naver/Upbit/Bithumb community action | Platform link or screenshot proof | Some platforms may require local accounts |

## Translation Guidance

Regional translation tasks should:

- preserve the original meaning;
- avoid adding unsupported claims;
- mention the source article when appropriate;
- stay neutral and informative;
- avoid investment or reward farming language.

## Description Examples

```text
I published a regional community post about QoreChain and submitted the public post link for manual review.
```

```text
I translated a QoreChain article into the requested language and submitted the article link.
```

```text
I shared QoreChain content in the requested regional community and provided a proof link for review.
```

## Risk Notes

- Do not post in communities where the topic is not allowed.
- Do not use misleading translations.
- Do not submit private chat screenshots with personal information.
- Do not present unofficial information as official.
