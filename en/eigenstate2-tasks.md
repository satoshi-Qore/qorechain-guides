# Eigenstate 2 Guide

## What is Eigenstate 2?

Eigenstate 2 is a task-based community program designed to increase participation, reward active users, and introduce the QoreChain ecosystem.

## Main Goals

- Grow the community
- Reward active contributors
- Increase ecosystem awareness
- Promote Mainnet and Light Node adoption

## Task Categories

- Social
- Educational
- Developer
- Exchanges
- On-Chain
- Korea/Asia
